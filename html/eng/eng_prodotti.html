Our Products

PANETTONE

Premium Classic

The first and most important ingredient is our passion for quality. Then there is the loving care we take of our sourdough starter, which we at Corsini have always treated as if it were our own child. Top-quality ingredients, adherence to the rules of the finest pastry-making tradition, meticulous and patient preparation, and the characteristic hand-shaping. Then there is the magic of the old family oven for the perfect bake, and finally... the wait: a long, natural rest for around 48 hours so that our Panettone can awaken even softer and more delicious. And so the Corsini Panettone is born, a small masterpiece of exquisite goodness, still made today just as it always was, and on which, since 1921, we have been proud to place our family’s signature.

CLASSIC PANETTONE

A classic naturally leavened panettone, light and fragrant, with raisins and candied orange and citron peel. A rich, aromatic dough with a balanced, authentic traditional flavour.

Almond Glazed

A soft, fragrant, naturally leavened artisan panettone, enriched with raisins and candied peel. Topped with a crunchy almond icing and sugar sprinkles, it has a rich, harmonious flavour.

Chocolate

A naturally leavened panettone without raisins or candied citrus fruit, featuring dark chocolate chips and a dark chocolate coating. Light and delicious, with an intense, rich flavour that will delight cocoa lovers.

Pears and Chocolate

A naturally leavened panettone without raisins or candied citrus fruit, featuring candied pears and chocolate chips. Light and fragrant, with a balanced flavour combining sweetness and rich notes.

Apricot

A soft and fragrant artisanal panettone made with natural yeast, enriched with 22% candied apricots. It has a delicate, well-balanced flavour, with sweet and fruity notes that enhance every bite.

PAN DI CORTE

SOURDOUGH MEETS THE REFINEMENT OF “TOSCANO IGP” EXTRA VIRGIN OLIVE OIL
“Toscano IGP” olive oil embodies regional character and a strong connection to the land and culture of our region, since 1988, it has been protected by the Protected Geographical Indications designation. From the combination with this refined ingredient comes Pan di Corte, a leavened cake with candied orange peel and sultanas, characterised by the absence of butter, for a more delicate yet equally delicious flavour.
